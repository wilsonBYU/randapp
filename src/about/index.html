About

Welcome to our Random Activity Website! This platform was developed with the aim of providing users with a diverse range of activities to break the monotony of daily life. The app will get the activities from boredapi and then will match with a picture from unsplash (or at least it will try).

Developer

This website is the brainchild of Wilson Romero, a dedicated and innovative developer. This project is a testament to his skills and knowledge gained from the Web Development course (WDD330) at Brigham Young University-Idaho.

Apis and Tools Used

The website utilizes several APIs and tools to deliver a unique and engaging user experience:

  • Images API:

    The captivating images you see on this site are sourced from Unsplash, a platform that offers high-resolution photos shared by a community of creative photographers.

  • Data API:

    The diverse range of activities suggested on this site are generated by BoredAPi, an API designed to provide random activities for those seeking something new and exciting to do.

  • Icons:

    The intuitive and aesthetically pleasing icons used throughout the site are provided by Font Awesome, a comprehensive library of icons for web developers and designers.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase the culmination of skills and knowledge acquired from the WDD330 course at BYU-I. It also aims to provide users with a fun and unique platform to discover new activities.

Thank you for visiting the site. I hope you find an activity that piques your interest!
